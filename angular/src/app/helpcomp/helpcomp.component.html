How can I create a reservation?
  • In the find a room page, search your location by city, state, building, floor, or by zip-code. Enter your event date and time. Enter the number of participants and the type of event. Click submit and results will appear based on your search paramters

Can I find the same space by using Outlook?
  • Yes, space using this tool can also be reserved using Outlook. However, the Room Portal application provides options to book the room without going directly into Outlook
What do I do if I am experiencing technical issues?
  • If you experience technical difficulties when using the Room Portal, call your local tech connections at 999-999-9999 or chat with your chat bot
What type of spaces are not reserved?
  • Focus Rooms: Enclosed spaces used for phone calls or one on one time
  • Resident Space: Seating assigned to employees who commute to the office 14 or more days a month
  • Unassigned Space: This consists of workstations, bench seating, offices, huddle rooms, and focus rooms. Spaces can be used without reservation and are available on first come first serve basis
